DO YOU WANT TO BECOME AN INTERNATIONAL STUDENT?

You want to study abroad, but you don’t know where to start... Or you might think that it’s too competitive and there is no chance you’re going to get it... Maybe, you are a displaced person and want to study in the country of your stay...

Don’t worry now – we’ve got you covered! With our help you will find the program of your dreams and let it come true. You can get the honest reviews from former and current international students, information about the admission requirements and all the tips and tricks you might need on your way to the dream!

We cover not only university mobilities, but programs you can apply for if you’re currently not enrolled in any educational institution. All information we’ve got is from official university sources and from the students’ experiences as well – only trustworthy sources!
